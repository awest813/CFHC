# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to **College Football Head Coach (CFHC)** and its official open-source builds maintained by Antdroid Interactive ("we", "our", or "the project").

## What information does CFHC collect?

CFHC does **not** require an account and does **not** collect personal information, analytics data, advertising identifiers, or precise device location data when you play the game.

## What data stays on your device?

CFHC stores gameplay data such as save files, imported league files, exported saves, and local settings on your device so the game can function offline.

That information stays on your device unless **you** choose to move, back up, export, or share those files using your operating system or another app.

## Does CFHC share data with third parties?

No. Because CFHC does not collect personal data from you, it does not sell or share personal data with third parties.

## Children's privacy

CFHC is not intended to knowingly solicit personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us at `contact@antdroid.dev` and we will review the issue.

## Security

Because CFHC is designed to run offline and does not transmit player data to our servers, the primary copies of your gameplay data remain under your control on your device or in any backups you create.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time by editing this file. Continued use of CFHC after a policy update means you accept the revised policy.

**Effective date:** 2026-04-27

## Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact `contact@antdroid.dev`.
